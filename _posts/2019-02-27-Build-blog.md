<img src="/assets/img/blogs/2019-02-27/1-1register.JPG" width="200px" />

## 2.建立仓库

登录后点后上角去创建一个存储库

<img src="/assets/img/blogs/2019-02-27/2-1.JPG" width="200px" />

<img src="/assets/img/blogs/2019-02-27/2-2.JPG" width="200px" />
